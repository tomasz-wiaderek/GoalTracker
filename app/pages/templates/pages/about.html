This is a non-commercial student project inspired by Quitzilla mobile app. Visit this page to check this awsome app.

GoalTracker is a web app that allows you to track your progress in fighting your bad habits.
Add a habit that you want to quit with and the app will track your progress and duration of abstinence. Milestones will be set automatically to keep you motivated.

Functionalities:

When logged out you can reset your password using your email. Email sender served by mailtrap.io.
Chose link My profile to view and/or update your Username and Email.
Chose link My habits to view list of all habits added by you and time of abstynence.

From My habits view you can also:

  • Add new habit
  • Update existing habit
  • Chagen name and description of your habit
  • Reset you habit - change the time of the last occurrance
  • See achieved and current milestone
  • Delete habit.

Additional features and improvements are under development. Any suggestions are most welcome.
